Lemon garlic salmon tray

  • Time: 11 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
salmon 200g
garlic 2 cloves
lemon half

Step 1: Mix the lemon garlic paste ingredients together
Step 2:Toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt and pepper.
Step 3:Oven grill / broil for 11 minutes or until the salmon is cooked.
