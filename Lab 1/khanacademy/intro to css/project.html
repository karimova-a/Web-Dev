Travel to Hawaii

Hawaii, the 50th U.S. state (admitted in 1959), is an archipelago in the central Pacific Ocean known for its volcanic landscape, tropical climate, and "Aloha" culture.
Located ~2,000 miles from the mainland, its major islands include Oʻahu (capital Honolulu), Maui, Kauaʻi, and the "Big Island" of Hawaiʻi.

Places to go to

  • Oahu
  • Hawaiʻi Volcanoes National Park
  • Hana Highway
